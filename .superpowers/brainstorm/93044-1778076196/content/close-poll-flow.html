Close Poll Flow

Step-by-step: what happens when the author selects the final date

1
Author taps a proposed date in the group calendar
Works in both list view and FullCalendar grid view. The date has a blue highlight and shows its acceptance counter (e.g. 4/6).
2
Action sheet opens — same as for any calevent
Zusagen / Absagen / Abstimmung anzeigen — plus one extra item visible only to the author or privileged users:
🔒 Diesen Termin wählen & Abstimmung schliessen
3
Author taps "Termin wählen & Abstimmung schliessen"
A confirmation alert appears with an optional closing message field:
Abstimmung schliessen
Sa, 22. Juni 2025 wird als definitiver Termin gewählt. Alle anderen Vorschläge werden entfernt.
Abbrechen
Bestätigen
4
Author confirms → store.closeSchedule(event, message)
Three Firestore writes in one batch:
UPDATESelected event: state → 'definitive', seriesId → ''
ARCHIVEAll other proposed events with same seriesId: isArchived → true
MATRIXSend message to group room (see step 5)
5
Matrix message sent to group chat room
Bruno Kaiser
✅ Vereins-Ausflug 2025
Termin: Samstag, 22. Juni 2025
Wir treffen uns wie besprochen. Freue mich auf euch!
heute, 14:32
6
Calendar refreshes
Selected date: blue → default colour (state: 'definitive'). All other proposed dates disappear from the calendar (isArchived). The event still shows the acceptance/decline list from the poll responses.
